Certificate Printing

The certificate printing portal by AASK maintains a continuous update system for all stakeholders.

The easy to use and minimalistic interface and real time dashboard enables the user to view the information he needs in seconds. The certificates are tailor made to suit the needs of the organization. The certificates are elegant to look and reflect your style.

The client will be in full control of managing the student’s information. The printing program will allow the client to upload the student’s information as a batch via excel upload feature. The program is capable of conducting content validation.

How certificate printing works.

Step 1

Step 2

Step 3

The system maps the registration id to put details and images together.

Step 4

Send for publication.
